A short Biography about Anand

Anand was born in small hometown of Nepal,Butwal. He was born on september which is quite sunny out there. Anand grew up playing with his younger sister because his parents were farmers and at that time it was difficult to look after both the children. In his childhood he visited India with his parents. He loves listening music and sometimes does reserch on different types of Automobiles.His hobby is playing cricket.

He completed his 10th garde from paramount boarding higher secondary school. On his school he used to be a bright student. He has won several medals on extra curricular activites held on his school days. He graduated his high school from New horizon english boarding school on 2016. He studied science on his high school which he felt quite difficult and passed on average marks.

At present he is a young, energetic, handsome guy and currently started living on Kathmandu(Capital city of Nepal) where he studies Bsc(hons) Computing, which is a british degree offered by Northamptom university to NAMI abbreviated as Naya Aayam Multi-disciplinary Institute in Nepal,Jorpati. In this way his journey is continuing on NAMI to be a Network Engineer.
